About Home Café

Home Café is a brand new cafe. The idea came during the Corona Virus pandemic. It's a new concept of “place”, which combines unique features under one roof. In Home Café you can enjoy:

  • - Delicious, home made food that is healthy and with high quality ingredients: healthy sandwiches, banana bread, omelet and many other delicious meals from our national cuisine;
  • - Smoothies and a big variety of fresh juices;
  • - Coffee, Frappe, Hot Chocolate, Milkshake and many many more;
  • - Shots and Cocktails with recipes, you can find only in our café
  • - Boarding Game nights and Music events;
